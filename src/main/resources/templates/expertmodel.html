Focus

 Focuses on the fuel consumption, power and price of the car. The higher Sum Index a car has, the more this car is cost-value (value-for-money!). Value Index focus on the power-value of a car. The higher Value Index, the higher power efficiency the car is, based on its price. Fuel Index focus on the 'fuel consumption-value' of a car. The higher Fuel Index, the better fuel comsumption efficiency the car is, based on the price.

Definition

 Sum Index is calculated by summing the car's respective' Value Index and the Fuel Index Value Index is calculated by 'Bhp / Price * 1000. It tells us the base horsepower of the car per SGD. Fuel Index is calculated by 'Fuel / Price * 100000'. It show us fuel efficiency of th car per SGD.
